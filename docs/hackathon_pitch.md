# VorteX WellMind – IBM watsonx Orchestrate Hackathon Pitch

## 1-Minute Elevator Pitch

**Problem**: 76% of employees experience burnout, costing companies $300B/year in lost productivity. HR teams react too late because they lack early warning systems.

**Solution**: **VorteX WellMind** – an AI-powered HR automation platform that detects burnout risk early and automatically triggers wellness interventions.

**How It Works**:
1. Employees take a 2-minute wellness survey
2. IBM watsonx.ai analyzes responses → burnout risk score (0-100)
3. Results stored in PostgreSQL + hash logged on Hedera blockchain for audit
4. Watson Orchestrate triggers automated workflows:
   - High risk → Email HR + create task
   - Urgent → Slack alert + schedule 1-on-1
   - Moderate → Send wellness resources
5. HR dashboard updates in real-time with charts, trends, blockchain verification

**Why We Win**:
- ✅ **Agentic AI**: watsonx makes decisions and triggers actions autonomously
- ✅ **Orchestration**: Watson Orchestrate automates 5+ HR workflows
- ✅ **Trust Layer**: Hedera blockchain ensures tamper-proof audit trail
- ✅ **Real Impact**: Prevent burnout → save $50K+ per employee in turnover costs

**Market**: $15B HR Tech market growing 10% annually. Target: Mid-to-large enterprises (500+ employees).

**Traction**: Prototype built in 48 hours, ready for pilot with 3 companies.
